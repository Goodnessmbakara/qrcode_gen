About QRocket

QRocket is a simple and efficient QR code generator built for businesses, developers, and everyday users. Whether you're sharing a link, contact info, or any other kind of data, QRocket makes it quick and easy.

The Idea Behind QRocket

I’ve always loved fiddling with computers growing up, spending most of my time exploring how they work. One day, I stumbled upon coding tutorials on YouTube, and that was when my interest in development sparked. I wanted to build something practical that anyone can use—hence, QRocket was born!

After doing some research, I joined the ALX programme to sharpen my skills, and this is the first project I’m proud to have worked on. I applied the knowledge I’ve gained so far, using languages and frameworks like Python and Django to bring QRocket to life.

Developer’s Journey

This project, QRocket, is my first major undertaking as a developer. Although I haven’t worked on any previous projects, I’ve put my heart and soul into this one. I contributed mainly by implementing the core backend functionality using the Django framework, allowing users to generate QR codes seamlessly.
